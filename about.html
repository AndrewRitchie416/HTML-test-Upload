A bit about Chef Share and it's Creator

An app to designed to share info and pictures between kitchen staff.

This app will provide multiple functions such as an area to type a message

(ie a prep list or shopping request) and take and send a picture (ie for sharing new recipes and platting designs)

-

Chef Share was created by Andrew Ritchie in 2018. After 10 years of working as a professional chef in multiple countries, Andrew

noticed a shift in how kitchen staff share information with their smart phones. A professional kitchen's natural instinct has always been to

share info via recipe books. In modern kitchens chefs are now gravitating towards using their phones for sharing work related information.

Logically there should be an app to support this, and so Chef Share was born.
